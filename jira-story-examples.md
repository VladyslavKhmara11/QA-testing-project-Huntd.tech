Test plan

Description

It is required to prepare a test plan for the functionality/project.
As part of this task, the following activities should be completed:

define the testing objectives and scope;

describe testing approaches, types, and levels;

identify in scope and out of scope areas;

define the test environment and required test data;

describe entry and exit criteria for testing;

identify risks, assumptions, and dependencies;

if necessary, define responsibilities and timelines.

The outcome of this task should be an up to date and approved test plan, ready for use during the testing process.


Main page(web)

Description

Contains info for recruiters about our product

This page should motivate recruiters to go to the list of candidates and sign up

The page should contain a comparison with other resources, quotes from the CEO, and logos of partners

Should contain a form for registration by email and social networks (Google, LinkedIn, GitHub), and links to Jobs and Web3 companies

For engineers' default page for all users

Authorized users should have a banner with the ad of the mobile app instead of a sign-up form

At the very bottom of the page, there should be at least 10 feedbacks from real users


Sign Up(web)

Description

"After entering the email and the password, there should be two options for how to continue registrations: as a recruiter or as a candidate.
Available APIs for registration: Google, LinkedIn, GitHub"

During the registration process, the recruiter should be able to fill his position, the company, and his contact information

One of the main stages should be filling in the information about the required candidates: the role of the candidate, required technologies, salary (annual/monthly), years of experience, English level, Timezone or country/city of the candidate

At the end of the registration process, the recruiter should be redirected to the list of candidates

"Stages of candidate registration:
Role: info about desired position, tech skills (5-15);
Expectations: info about the experience, expected salary (annual or monthly, dollar or euro), English level, and location;
Experience: detailed information about experience, should be possible to export from LinkedIn;
Bio: info about the achievements of the candidate and expectations from work;
Contact Information: avatar, full name, CV to upload, and links to social networks"

After successful registration, users should wait for profile activation by Admins during the next 24-48 hours


Test report

Description

It is required to prepare a test report for the functionality/project.
As part of this task, the following activities should be completed:

collect and analyze test execution results;

summarize performed testing activities;

provide information on test coverage and scope;

document identified defects and their current status;

The outcome of this task should be a complete and up-to-date test report, suitable for stakeholders’ review and decision-making.
